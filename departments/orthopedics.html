Overview

Advanced Bone & Joint Care

The Department of Orthopedics at Cherish Hospital provides world-class treatment for all musculoskeletal conditions. Our specialists are trained at leading institutions and use the latest computer-navigated and robotic-assisted surgical techniques.

Whether it's a sports injury, degenerative joint disease, or a complex fracture, our comprehensive approach combines surgical expertise with dedicated physiotherapy to ensure the fastest recovery possible.

Conditions We Treat

Musculoskeletal Conditions

Osteoarthritis

Degenerative joint disease causing pain and stiffness, treated with therapy, injections or joint replacement.

Sports Injuries

ACL tears, meniscus injuries, rotator cuff tears and stress fractures in athletes.

Fractures & Trauma

Emergency fracture management including complex pelvic and spinal fractures.

Carpal Tunnel Syndrome

Nerve compression in the wrist causing numbness, treated with splinting or surgery.

Spinal Disc Problems

Herniated discs, sciatica and spinal stenosis managed with conservative or surgical approaches.

Pediatric Orthopedics

Clubfoot, growth plate injuries and scoliosis treatment in children.

Treatments & Procedures

Surgical Excellence

Total Knee Replacement

Robotic-assisted precision surgery for severe knee arthritis with rapid recovery protocols.

Total Hip Replacement

Minimally invasive approach for hip joint restoration using advanced prosthetics.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Keyhole surgery for joint repair — faster healing, minimal scarring.

Fracture Fixation

Internal fixation with plates, screws and intramedullary nails for complex fractures.
