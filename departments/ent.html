Overview

Precision ENT Care

The ENT Department at Cherish Hospital offers complete diagnostic and surgical care for disorders of the ear, nose, throat, head and neck. Our team uses endoscopic technology and microsurgery techniques for minimally invasive treatment.

From hearing loss and voice disorders to sleep apnea and head-neck tumours, our specialists provide evidence-based care with excellent outcomes.

Conditions We Treat

ENT Conditions

Hearing Loss

Complete audiological evaluation and treatment including hearing aids and cochlear implants.

Chronic Sinusitis

Medical and surgical management of recurrent sinus infections including endoscopic sinus surgery.

Sleep Apnea & Snoring

Sleep study evaluation and surgical correction including UPPP and septoplasty.

Voice Disorders

Vocal cord evaluation with stroboscopy and treatment for hoarseness and voice changes.

Tonsillitis & Adenoids

Recurrent tonsillitis treatment including tonsillectomy and adenoidectomy in children and adults.

Vertigo & Balance

Diagnosis and management of dizziness, vertigo and balance disorders.

Procedures

Advanced ENT Procedures

Endoscopic Sinus Surgery (FESS)

Minimally invasive correction of chronic sinusitis and nasal polyps.

Cochlear Implant Surgery

Restoring hearing in profoundly deaf patients through electronic implant devices.

Septoplasty

Surgical correction of deviated nasal septum to improve breathing and reduce snoring.

Micro-Ear Surgery

Tympanoplasty and mastoidectomy for chronic ear infections and hearing restoration.

Common Questions

Visit an ENT if you have persistent ear pain, hearing loss, chronic nasal congestion, recurrent sore throat, difficulty swallowing, or persistent hoarseness lasting more than 2 weeks.

Modern endoscopic sinus surgery is minimally invasive with minimal post-operative pain. Most patients experience only mild discomfort and return to normal activities within a week.

Depending on the cause, hearing can often be improved with surgery, hearing aids, or cochlear implants. Early intervention gives the best results, especially in children.
