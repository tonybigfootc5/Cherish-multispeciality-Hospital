Overview

Heart Care You Can Trust

The Department of Cardiology at Cherish Hospital is a centre of excellence for treating the full spectrum of cardiovascular diseases. Our state-of-the-art catheterization labs, non-invasive cardiac imaging suites, and dedicated Cardiac ICU ensure patients receive the highest standard of care.

From preventive cardiology and risk assessment to complex interventional procedures like angioplasty and pacemaker implantation, our team of internationally trained cardiologists delivers outcomes that match the best in the world.

Conditions We Treat

Comprehensive Cardiac Conditions

Coronary Artery Disease

Blockages in heart arteries causing chest pain, managed with medication, stents or bypass surgery.

Arrhythmias

Irregular heartbeat disorders treated with medication, ablation therapy or pacemaker devices.

Hypertension

High blood pressure management with lifestyle modification and targeted drug therapy.

Heart Failure

Advanced treatment including device therapy, cardiac rehabilitation and transplant referral.

Valvular Heart Disease

Repair and replacement of damaged heart valves using minimally invasive techniques.

Congenital Heart Defects

Diagnosis and treatment of structural heart problems present from birth.

Treatments & Procedures

Advanced Procedures

Coronary Angioplasty & Stenting

Minimally invasive procedure to open blocked arteries using balloon and stent placement.

Pacemaker Implantation

Permanent device placement to regulate heart rhythm in patients with bradycardia.

Cardiac Ablation

Precise destruction of abnormal heart tissue causing irregular rhythms.

Echocardiography

Advanced ultrasound imaging of the heart for accurate diagnosis and monitoring.
