Overview

Child-Friendly Healthcare

Our Pediatrics Department provides comprehensive medical care in a warm, child-friendly environment. From newborn screening and vaccinations to complex pediatric surgeries, we ensure every child receives age-appropriate, evidence-based treatment.

Our dedicated Pediatric ICU (PICU) and Neonatal ICU (NICU) are equipped with the latest life-support technology, staffed round the clock by experienced neonatologists and pediatric intensivists.
