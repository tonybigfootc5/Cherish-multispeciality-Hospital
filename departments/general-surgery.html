Overview

Surgical Excellence, Compassionate Care

The Department of General Surgery at Cherish Hospital is the hospital's flagship department, led by our Founder & Director Dr. Indra Mohan. With over 20 years of clinical experience and 5000+ successful surgeries, the department is renowned for its expertise in both laparoscopic (keyhole) and open surgical procedures.

Our state-of-the-art modular operation theatres with laminar airflow, advanced energy devices, and HD laparoscopic systems ensure the highest precision and safety standards for every patient.

Conditions We Treat

Surgical Conditions

Appendicitis

Emergency and elective laparoscopic appendectomy with same-day discharge in uncomplicated cases.

Gallstones

Laparoscopic cholecystectomy for gallbladder stones — keyhole surgery with minimal scarring.

Hernias

All types of hernia repair — inguinal, umbilical, incisional — using mesh-based laparoscopic techniques.

Gastrointestinal Disorders

Surgical management of ulcers, intestinal obstruction, diverticulitis and GI bleeding.

Surgical Oncology

Tumour excision, biopsies and cancer surgery for breast, thyroid, and abdominal malignancies.

Trauma Surgery

Emergency surgical care for road accidents, falls, stab injuries and blunt abdominal trauma.
