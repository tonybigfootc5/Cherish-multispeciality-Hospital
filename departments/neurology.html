Overview

Expert Neurological Care

The Neurology Department at Cherish Hospital is equipped with advanced neuro-diagnostic technology including high-resolution MRI, EEG monitoring, and nerve conduction studies. Our team of neurologists and neurosurgeons treats the complete spectrum of neurological conditions.

From headaches and epilepsy to complex brain tumours and spinal cord injuries, we combine advanced technology with personalized treatment plans for the best possible outcomes.

Common Questions

Remember FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time to call emergency. If you notice any of these signs, seek immediate medical help.

Many patients achieve seizure-free status with proper medication. In drug-resistant cases, epilepsy surgery can offer a cure in up to 70% of carefully selected patients.

Consult a neurologist if you have frequent headaches (more than 2-3 per week), sudden severe headache, headaches with vision changes, or headaches that don't respond to over-the-counter medicines.
