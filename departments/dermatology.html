Overview

Healthy Skin, Confident You

The Dermatology Department at Cherish Hospital combines medical dermatology with the latest cosmetic procedures. From treating chronic skin conditions to aesthetic enhancements, our specialists use evidence-based approaches and FDA-approved technologies.

We offer comprehensive skin health management including allergy testing, laser treatments, hair restoration and anti-aging therapies in a comfortable clinical setting.

Conditions We Treat

Skin & Hair Conditions

Acne & Acne Scars

Medical treatment, chemical peels, microneedling and laser therapy for acne and scar reduction.

Eczema & Psoriasis

Long-term management with biologics, phototherapy and topical treatments.

Pigmentation Disorders

Melasma, vitiligo and sun damage treated with lasers, peels and targeted therapies.

Hair Loss (Alopecia)

PRP therapy, minoxidil treatment, and hair transplant counselling for pattern baldness.

Allergic Skin Reactions

Patch testing, contact dermatitis management and urticaria treatment.

Fungal Infections

Treatment for ringworm, athlete's foot, nail fungus and other dermatophyte infections.
