Overview

Comprehensive Women's Health

Our Gynecology & Obstetrics Department is dedicated to women's wellbeing at every stage of life. From adolescent health and pregnancy care to menopause management and gynecological oncology, we provide a safe and supportive environment.

Our labour and delivery suites are designed for comfort, with advanced fetal monitoring, and our team is experienced in both normal and high-risk pregnancies.

Conditions & Services

Women's Health Services

High-Risk Pregnancy

Specialised monitoring for gestational diabetes, preeclampsia, multiple pregnancies and previous C-section pregnancies.

Normal & C-Section Delivery

Safe delivery with modern labour rooms, epidural analgesia, and neonatal support team on standby.

Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive fertility assessment and treatment including IUI, hormonal therapy and surgical correction.

PCOD / PCOS Management

Hormonal evaluation and lifestyle-based management of polycystic ovarian syndrome.

Gynecological Oncology

Screening and treatment for cervical, ovarian and uterine cancers with multidisciplinary approach.

Menopause Clinic

Hormone replacement therapy, bone density screening and wellness programmes for menopausal women.

Procedures

Surgical Procedures

Laparoscopic Hysterectomy

Minimally invasive uterus removal with faster recovery and less scarring.

Fibroid Removal

Myomectomy using laparoscopic or hysteroscopic approach to preserve fertility.

Hysteroscopy

Diagnostic and operative hysteroscopy for uterine polyps, septa and adhesions.

Pap Smear & HPV Screening

Routine cervical cancer screening for early detection and prevention.
